ABOUT

WeCliff

WeCliff International Our multinational trading team has demonstrated partners from around the world our import and export team traveled the world to develop relationships with suppliers. we continue to meet face to face with many of our suppliers and customers. Building relationships and trust form the foundation of our company. You can rely on us to navigate through the vast maze of document requirements for product import and export. Our services cover the smallest detail to ensure the most efficient transport and easy access to international markets.

When we work together, we take the time to understand our clients’ requirements and work with our producers to get the shipment quickly and efficiently to its destination. We handle the shipment needs, and destination requirements such as legal requirements, product clearance, permit applications, logistical solutions, storage or managing foreign exchange, we do it all. We go to great lengths to understand our clients’ needs. We look at the product and consider seasonality, functionally, destination, and demand forecasts. The considerations then determine where the product is sourced and how to best manage the supply chain.

Our guiding principles are integrity, respect, teamwork, achievement and innovation. These key principles describe our culture and help us achieve our vision. We are passionate about our clients and back it up with the best service. Our team members are the heart and soul of our business. As a socially responsible company, we pursue sustainability within our operations by striving to be environmentally aware and economically viable.
